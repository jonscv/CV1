About

Hi, as a family chef of 50 years, I've drawn on many influences including my Mauritian and Chinese heritage, love for indian and spanish food, and a natural push to refine and perfect my dishes. It's been a long and arduous journey of learning, experimentation and refinement , to now bring you quality food that will simply blow your taste buds away.

I'm now in a position to share all this with those closest to me - "My neighbours and fellow residents of Sellincourt Road, I welcome you to the Tooting Gourmet Kitchen"

If you'd like to follow me on Twitter, my username is masterkilla.
